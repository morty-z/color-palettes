# Solarized

Solarized is a precision-crafted color scheme by Ethan Schoonover, designed around careful CIELAB relationships to reduce eye strain while maintaining readability. Its warm, parchment-toned light palette paired with muted teal and orange accents creates a scholarly, inviting atmosphere. Ideal for documentation, technical writing, and developer-focused content.

- **Mode**: light
- **Font category**: sans-serif
- **Best for**: technical documentation, developer blogs, README pages, knowledge bases
