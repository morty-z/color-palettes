# WeChat

Fresh, friendly, and functional. Inspired by the WeChat ecosystem and its official articles platform, this theme uses a vibrant green accent on a clean white canvas. It suits content targeting Chinese social media audiences, newsletter-style articles, and mobile-first reading experiences.

- **Mode**: light
- **Font category**: sans-serif
- **Best for**: social media articles, newsletters, mobile reading, WeChat official accounts
