# Apple

Clean, minimalist, and precision-crafted. Inspired by Apple's Human Interface Guidelines and apple.com editorial pages, this theme emphasizes generous whitespace, subtle typography, and a refined blue accent. Best suited for product pages, developer documentation, and polished marketing content.

- **Mode**: light
- **Font category**: sans-serif
- **Best for**: product pages, developer docs, marketing sites, corporate communications
