# Lavender

Lavender is a soft, dreamy theme built around gentle purple tones that evoke calm sophistication and creative warmth. Its light violet background and rounded corners give it a modern, approachable feel without sacrificing readability. Ideal for personal blogs, creative writing platforms, lifestyle content, and wellness-focused products.

- **Mode**: light
- **Font category**: sans-serif
- **Best for**: personal blogs, creative writing, lifestyle content, wellness products, design portfolios
