# Coffee

Coffee is a warm, inviting theme inspired by the cozy ambiance of a cafe with rich brown tones and cream-colored paper. Its serif typography and earthy palette evoke the feeling of reading a well-loved book over a cup of espresso. The subtle radial gradient background adds organic warmth without distraction. Ideal for food blogs, literary journals, recipe sites, and lifestyle brands.

- **Mode**: light
- **Font category**: serif
- **Best for**: food blogs, recipe sites, literary journals, lifestyle brands, cafe and restaurant sites
