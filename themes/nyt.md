# NYT

Authoritative, elegant, and editorial. Inspired by The New York Times digital reading experience, this theme uses serif typography on a warm cream background with steel blue accents. It conveys journalistic credibility and is perfect for long-form articles, opinion pieces, and literary content.

- **Mode**: light
- **Font category**: serif
- **Best for**: long-form articles, journalism, opinion pieces, literary content, editorial blogs
