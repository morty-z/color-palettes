# Ocean

Ocean is a deep, immersive dark theme inspired by the midnight sea, pairing a dark navy background with vibrant coral, gold, and blue accents that evoke bioluminescent ocean life. Each heading level uses a distinct warm-to-cool color, creating a dramatic and engaging visual hierarchy. Great for creative projects, landing pages, and media-rich content that wants a bold, atmospheric feel.

- **Mode**: dark
- **Font category**: sans-serif
- **Best for**: creative projects, landing pages, media-rich content, portfolios, gaming sites
