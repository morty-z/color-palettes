# Feishu

Modern, collaborative, and efficient. Inspired by Feishu (Lark) by ByteDance, this theme uses a soft grey background with a bold blue accent that feels productive and organized. It is well-suited for internal documentation, team wikis, project notes, and enterprise collaboration content.

- **Mode**: light
- **Font category**: sans-serif
- **Best for**: internal docs, team wikis, project notes, enterprise collaboration, knowledge management
