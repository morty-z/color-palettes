# Notion

Notion's style is clean, minimal, and content-first, inspired by the popular productivity app. It uses a warm brown-black palette on a pure white background with generous line-height and tight spacing between elements, creating a distraction-free writing surface. Best suited for documentation, wikis, personal notes, and knowledge bases.

- **Mode**: light
- **Font category**: sans-serif
- **Best for**: documentation, wikis, personal notes, knowledge bases, internal tools
