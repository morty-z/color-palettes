# Claude

Warm, thoughtful, and approachable. Inspired by Anthropic's Claude interface, this theme pairs a parchment-toned background with a distinctive terracotta accent that conveys intelligence and warmth. Ideal for AI-related content, knowledge bases, and editorial writing that values readability.

- **Mode**: light
- **Font category**: sans-serif
- **Best for**: AI products, knowledge bases, editorial content, documentation
