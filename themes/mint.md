# Mint

Mint is a fresh, nature-inspired light theme built on cool greens and teals, evoking the feeling of a morning garden. Its soft mint background paired with deep forest-green text creates excellent readability without the starkness of pure black-on-white. Well suited for health and wellness apps, environmental projects, and any content that benefits from a calm, refreshing visual tone.

- **Mode**: light
- **Font category**: sans-serif
- **Best for**: health and wellness apps, environmental projects, recipe sites, garden blogs, eco-friendly brands
