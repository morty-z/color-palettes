# Sspai

Sspai's style is editorial, bold, and reading-focused, inspired by the popular Chinese tech media platform known for long-form articles. It features a striking red accent color for headings and highlights, generous line-height for comfortable reading, and a distinctive left-bordered h2 treatment. Perfect for blogs, editorial content, and long-form articles.

- **Mode**: light
- **Font category**: sans-serif
- **Best for**: blogs, editorial content, long-form articles, tech reviews, newsletters
