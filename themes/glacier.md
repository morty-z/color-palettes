# Glacier

Glacier is a clean, icy-blue theme that channels the crisp clarity of arctic landscapes. Its cool-toned palette with a faintly blue-tinted background provides a professional, trustworthy feel without being cold or sterile. The range of blues from deep navy to bright sky creates a cohesive, calming visual flow. Suited for corporate sites, SaaS dashboards, and data-heavy applications.

- **Mode**: light
- **Font category**: sans-serif
- **Best for**: corporate sites, SaaS products, dashboards, data-heavy applications, business documentation
