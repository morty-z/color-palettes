# Forest

Forest is a dark, nature-inspired theme that immerses readers in deep woodland greens. Its muted, earthy palette simulates the experience of reading by lantern light under a canopy of trees. The leaf-green accents on a dark green background provide comfortable contrast for extended reading. Best for environmental projects, outdoor brands, and nature-themed content.

- **Mode**: dark
- **Font category**: sans-serif
- **Best for**: environmental projects, outdoor brands, nature blogs, sustainability content, green-tech dashboards
