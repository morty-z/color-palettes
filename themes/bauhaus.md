# Bauhaus

Bauhaus is a bold, geometric theme inspired by the legendary German art school's philosophy of form meeting function. It uses the iconic primary color triad -- red, blue, and yellow -- against stark white with heavy black type to create a striking, authoritative visual language. The uppercase headings, sharp zero-radius corners, and thick borders channel constructivist graphic design. Perfect for design portfolios, architecture firms, and art-focused publications.

- **Mode**: light
- **Font category**: sans-serif
- **Best for**: design portfolios, architecture firms, art publications, creative agencies, museum sites
