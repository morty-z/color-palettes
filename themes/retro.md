# Retro

Nostalgic, textured, and distinguished. Inspired by vintage print design and old-world typography, this theme uses a parchment background with a seal-wax red accent and serif fonts. It evokes the feel of aged paper and letterpress printing, making it ideal for literary magazines, historical content, and artisan brand storytelling.

- **Mode**: light
- **Font category**: serif
- **Best for**: literary magazines, historical content, artisan brands, vintage aesthetics, print-inspired layouts
