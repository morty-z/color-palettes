# Medium

Open, readable, and content-first. Inspired by Medium's celebrated reading experience, this theme prioritizes generous line heights and paragraph spacing with a restrained green accent. It creates an immersive reading flow that keeps the focus entirely on the writing, making it ideal for blogs and essays.

- **Mode**: light
- **Font category**: sans-serif
- **Best for**: blogs, essays, personal writing, storytelling, content-first platforms
