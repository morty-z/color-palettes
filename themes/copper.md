# Copper

Copper is a dark, warm-toned theme that evokes the rich patina of aged copper and burnished leather. Its deep chocolate background paired with warm metallic accents creates an atmosphere of understated luxury and timeless elegance. The serif typography reinforces the classic, refined character. Ideal for whiskey brands, premium product pages, luxury portfolios, and vintage-inspired designs.

- **Mode**: dark
- **Font category**: serif
- **Best for**: luxury brands, premium product pages, whiskey and spirits sites, vintage portfolios, leather goods
