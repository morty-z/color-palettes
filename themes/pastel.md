# Pastel

Pastel is a playful, multi-hued theme that uses soft candy colors to create a cheerful, welcoming atmosphere. Each heading level has its own pastel accent -- pink, blue, green, and amber -- producing a rainbow hierarchy that feels lighthearted without becoming chaotic. The generous border radii and gradient hr add to its friendly character. Perfect for children's content, creative projects, personal journals, and whimsical portfolios.

- **Mode**: light
- **Font category**: sans-serif
- **Best for**: children's content, personal journals, creative projects, whimsical portfolios, event sites
