# Ink

Ink is a stark, black-and-white typographic theme that strips away all color to let content speak through form alone. Inspired by traditional print design and letterpress aesthetics, it relies on weight, spacing, and classical serif typography to create hierarchy. Suited for literary magazines, essays, long-form writing, and minimalist portfolios.

- **Mode**: light
- **Font category**: serif
- **Best for**: literary blogs, essays, long-form articles, minimalist portfolios, print-inspired layouts
