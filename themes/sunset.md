# Sunset

Sunset is a warm, literary theme that channels the golden hour with its cream background, burnt orange accents, and serif typography. The combination of Georgia font and generous 1.8 line-height creates a classic, book-like reading experience with italic blockquotes adding an editorial touch. Perfect for essays, storytelling, literary blogs, and any content that values a warm, timeless aesthetic.

- **Mode**: light
- **Font category**: serif
- **Best for**: essays, storytelling, literary blogs, book reviews, magazines, editorial content
